LTD BASILON GLOBAL SERVICE

What we do:

Ltd "Basilon Global Service" is a construction company which was founded in 2011, Tbilisi. Our fields of work are construction, hotel management and bio-organic agriculture.

Construction:

Our successfull projects:
  • Multifunctional buildings on Aleksidze #1, Aleksidze #7 and Tkvarcheli #2.
  • We are currently working on a large-scale project named "Basilon Towers".
  • We are also working on resort "Basilon Eco Resort" in Sioni, which will give both residents ang guests of Tbilisi an opportunity to relax and replenish energy amidst the nature's depths at the sea level of 1050m.

Hotel:

LTD "Basilon Global Service" owns and operates "Basilon Hotel" which was opened in 2014 in the district of Saburtalo. The company also currently works on a new hotel on Tkvarcheli 2.

Bio-organic agriculture:

Our current project encompasses the production and cultivation of bio-organic agricultural products (pomegranate, Saperavi and Aleksandrouli vines.) Our projects stand out with the sheer scale, diversity, innovativative approach, modern architecture and mostly with quality because of which customers have been choosing "Basilon Global Service" for years.
